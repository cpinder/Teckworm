Welcome to Teckworm.com

Hello, my name is Chris, and I’m the owner of Teckworm.com. Thanks so much for stopping by — I’m glad you’re here, and I hope you find the site genuinely useful.

I created Teckworm for a few key reasons:

  • I’ve completed a number of IT certifications over the years and developed a reliable, repeatable system for preparing and passing them. I want to share those strategies with others who are working toward the same goals.
  • I’m passionate about ITIL methodology and enjoy meaningful conversations about how ITIL practices are applied in real environments. My focus is not just on theory, but on practical, real‑world implementation.
  • I’ve spent many years working on an IT service desk, and I want to pass along the lessons, insights, and best practices that can help others succeed in their own IT careers.

Thanks again for visiting — I’m excited to share what I’ve learned and connect with others in the IT community.
